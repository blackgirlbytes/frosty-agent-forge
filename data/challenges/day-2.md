## The Crisis

The festival's beloved storyteller has lost their voice to laryngitis, but the interactive storytelling booth is supposed to open in an hour! Fifty excited children are already lining up outside, and their parents are getting restless.

## Your Mission

Build a choose-your-own-adventure web app that brings the storyteller's magic to life. The app should:

- Present an engaging winter-themed story
- Offer meaningful choices that affect the narrative
- Include multiple possible endings
- Feature festive winter styling
- Work seamlessly on both desktop and mobile

## What You'll Learn

- **Conversational Development**: Build a complete application through natural conversation with goose
- **Developer Extension**: Watch goose use tools transparently (file creation, shell commands)
- **Interactive UIs**: Create engaging user experiences with branching logic
- **Rapid Prototyping**: Go from idea to working app in minutes
